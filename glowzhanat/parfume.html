The Camellia Deep Collagen Firming Ampoule deeply nourishes and firms the skin with a powerful blend of camellia flower extract and hydrolyzed collagen. Designed to restore elasticity and enhance skin resilience, it absorbs quickly to deliver long-lasting hydration and visible plumping effects. The lightweight yet deeply moisturizing formula helps improve skin texture, leaving it firmer, smoother, and more radiant. Dermatologically tested, this ampoule is ideal for those seeking a youthful, lifted appearance.

How to use

Use it after cleansing and toner: apply a few drops to the face and neck, spread evenly, and pat gently until absorbed. Follow with moisturizer, and in the morning always apply sunscreen.

Ingredients

Water, Camellia Japonica Flower Extract, Glycerin, Methylpropanediol, Niacinamide, 1,2-Hexanediol, Betaine, Hydroxyacetophenone, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Arginine, Tromethamine, Panthenol, Glyceryl Acrylate/Acrylic Acid Copolymer, Hydrolyzed Collagen, Beta-Glucan, Allantoin, Adenosine, Ethylhexylglycerin, Disodium EDTA, Squalane, Jojoba Seed Oil, Ceramide NP, Cholesterol, Hydrogenated Lecithin, Tocopherol, Quercetin, Hydroxypinacolone Retinoate.

What Skin Type

It works well for normal, dry, combination, and sensitive skin. It is especially good for aging or tired skin that needs more firmness and hydration. Oily and acne-prone skin can also use it, but it’s best to test first.
